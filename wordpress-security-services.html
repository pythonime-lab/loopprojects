Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my WordPress site has been hacked?

Common signs include: unexpected redirects to unknown sites, Google showing a "This site may be hacked" warning, your hosting provider suspending your account, visitors seeing spam content you didn't create, or your admin password suddenly not working.

How long does the malware removal take?

Most cleanups are completed within 24 to 48 hours from the moment work begins. Complex infections may take up to 72 hours. You'll receive a progress update if anything changes.

Will my site go offline during the cleanup?

In most cases, no. The cleanup is done on the live files and database with minimal disruption. For very severe infections a short maintenance window may be needed — you'll be informed in advance.

What if the malware comes back after the cleanup?

If the same infection reappears within 7 days of service completion, I'll re-clean your site at no extra cost. The Cleanup + Protection plan also includes hardening measures that significantly reduce the risk of reinfection.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?

No. I'll guide you step by step through providing the necessary access. You don't need to understand the technical side — that's what I'm here for.

Is there a free consultation?

Yes. A free 15-minute consultation is available to assess your situation before any commitment. Fill in the contact form and I'll get back to you within 48 hours.
